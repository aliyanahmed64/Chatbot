Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to create a new password.
Can I use ChatAssist on mobile devices?
Yes! ChatAssist is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets. You can also download our mobile app for iOS and Android devices.
How do I upgrade my subscription plan?
Log into your account, go to Settings > Subscription, and click on "Upgrade Plan" to see available options and complete your upgrade.
Is my data secure with ChatAssist?
We take security seriously. All conversations are encrypted, and we adhere to strict privacy policies. Your data is never shared with third parties without your consent.
